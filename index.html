> about us

we are a public UNH student org centered around programming. most meetings consist of breaking down leet-code style problems and attempting to solve them in group settings. we put a heavy focus towards teamwork as opposed to going solo, since collaboration is something we feel is sorely missing from our coursework.

weekly meetings every thursday, 5:30-7:00pm, in Kingsbury N204. (above Alberts' Cafe)

join our discord for more information & announcements.
